Blabl

Design • Development

Launching a Children's Speech Pathology App

As part of the DALI Lab, a tech consultancy at Dartmouth, I worked on a speech pathology startup called Blabl. As the sole designer and iOS developer, I inherited an iPad game that needed design modifications and a more dynamic system of user retention.

Over the course of 10 weeks, I designed, prototyped, and coded a virtual store feature to fully gamify the user experience. I also revamped the app's home screen and created a landing page for the startup.
