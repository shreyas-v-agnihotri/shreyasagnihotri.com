Habit Tracker

Designing an App for Mindfulness

As the culminating project for my UI/UX design course, I was tasked with conceptualizing and designing an app to improve mindfulness. Together with a partner, I progressed through the entire design process — conducting user interviews, creating personas and journey maps, sketching ideas, building low-fi and hi-fi mockups, and prototyping.

The final product is an app called Habit Tracker that encourages users to develop strong habits through historical metrics, accountability systems, and positive reinforcement.
